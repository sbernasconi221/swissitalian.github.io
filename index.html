In the Life Of a Swiss-Italian

We are almost like Italians, just more organized.

Food:

Lots of pasta
Really like pasta with vegetables such as zucchini
Lots of soup
All types of vegetable soup is loved everyday
But a personal favorite is just orzo pasta with broth, onions and potatoes!

Specialties:

CHEESE
CHOCOLATE
Wherever you go in Switzerland you are bound to find a cow, that is why most dairy products are our specialty.
Another specialty is our cleanliness, wherever you go it is clean... we just have common decency.

Activites:

Hiking is one of my favorite things to do in Switzerland. But a local favorite is to just relax.
Barbequing and spending time together is one of the most common activities to do in Switzerland.
Also these pools called Lidos are everywhere.
They are just public pools where families go to spend time outside together.

Language:

Obviously, as a Swiss Italian, we speak Italian, but there are many more languages spoken in Switzerland.
Besides Italian there is also French, German, Romansh, and many dialects between those 4.

Random facts:

Water and food is just better in Switzerland, no arguing.
Their big grocery stores are called Coop. Not only do they sell groceries but it is a restaurant, furniture, home accessories, clothing store, and a salon. They are always multiple stories high.

Overall I wish I was in Switzerland
